## 🗺️ MAPS OPTIMIZATION CHECKLIST

### Setup Required
- [ ] Google My Business — Address, phone, hours, photos, description
- [ ] Google Maps — Same info synced automatically from GMB
- [ ] Apple Maps — Submit business to Apple Maps Connect
- [ ] Yandex Maps — Claim business, add photos + info (Russia CIS traffic)
- [ ] Baidu Maps — Register on Baidu, submit address (China traffic)
- [ ] HERE Maps — List business on HERE
- [ ] OpenStreetMap — Add hostel as a node/amenity
- [ ] Foursquare — Claim venue, add check-in specials

### Optimization Tips
1. **Consistent NAP:** Name, Address, Phone identical across ALL platforms
2. **Photos:** Upload 10+ high-quality exterior/interior photos (Google, Apple, Baidu)
3. **Hours:** Update opening hours on ALL maps (matches booking pages)
4. **Categories:** Tag as "Hostel" + "Budget Accommodation" + "Backpacker" where available
5. **Description:** 150+ words about facility, amenities, location benefits
6. **Reviews:** Encourage guests to leave reviews on Google Maps (highest weight)
7. **Posts:** Use Google My Business posts feature (updates appear on Maps)
8. **Q&A:** Answer guest questions on Google Maps Q&A section
9. **Attributes:** Tag: WiFi, AC, Lockers, Kitchen, Hot Water, Parking, Breakfast
10. **Service Areas:** Set "Varanasi" as primary service area

### Expected Impact
- **Google Maps:** 20-30% of local discovery traffic
- **Apple Maps:** 10-15% (iOS users, especially international)
- **Yandex:** 2-5% (Russia/CIS tourists)
- **Baidu:** 3-8% (Chinese tourist segment)
