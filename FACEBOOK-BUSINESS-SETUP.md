## PHASE 3: Content Strategy (Ongoing)

### Post Types (2-3x per week)

**Type 1: Room Showcase (Monday)**
- Upload high-quality room photos
- Caption: Room type, bed count, price range, amenities
- Link to booking page

**Type 2: Amenities/Updates (Wednesday)**
- Rooftop view, common areas, kitchen, lounge
- Highlight special events or new additions
- Encourage check-in/booking

**Type 3: Guest Stories (Friday)**
- Testimonials from guests
- Guest reviews/quotes
- Photos of happy guests
- Tag guests (if they allow)

**Type 4: Local Highlights**
- Varanasi attractions (Assi Ghat, ghats, temples)
- Local food recommendations
- Travel tips for first-time visitors
- Backpacker guides

### Post Strategy
- **Optimal Times:** 9-11 AM, 6-8 PM (India time)
- **Hashtags:** #VaranasiHostel #BackpackerLife #Varanasi #HostelInVaranasi #BudgetTravel #IndiaTravel
- **Call-to-Action:** Every post should encourage booking or messaging
- **Length:** Short captions (2-3 sentences) with emoji
