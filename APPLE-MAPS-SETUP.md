## Troubleshooting

**Issue:** Cannot find business in Apple Maps  
**Solution:** Create new listing. Click "Add your business" → search address → "Not listed?" → create new

**Issue:** Verification taking too long  
**Solution:** Try alternative verification method (phone vs postcard vs video)

**Issue:** Can't claim - "Already claimed by someone"  
**Solution:** 
1. Click "Not your business?"
2. Request removal with proof of ownership
3. Re-claim once removed

**Issue:** Photos not uploading  
**Solution:** Check file size (<10MB), format (JPG/PNG), and dimensions (300x300+ px)
